Designer Profile
我是一名致力于创新设计和可持续设计的设计师。我擅长交互设计、平面设计、动效设计、服务设计，并能够胜任创意写作和活动策划等多领域工作。我的作品集涵盖多个年度项目，通过清晰的时间线展现设计思路和创新成果。我注重用简练而清晰的设计语言表达想法，尤其擅长从个人视角阐释项目背后的逻辑与价值。作为一位既专注于细节又善于从全局出发的设计师，我始终追求用设计驱动可持续发展和用户体验提升。 I am a designer dedicated to innovative and sustainable design. I specialize in interaction design, graphic design, motion design, and service design, and I am also proficient in creative writing and event planning across multiple fields. My portfolio spans multiple years of projects, showcasing my design thinking and innovative achievements through a clear timeline. I focus on expressing ideas with concise and clear design language, particularly excelling at articulating the logic and value behind projects from a personal perspective. As a designer who pays close attention to details while maintaining a holistic view, I am committed to driving sustainable development and enhancing user experience through design.
